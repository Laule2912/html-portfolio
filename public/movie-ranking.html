The lastest 3 movies I have watched (07/2023).

Transformers Rise of the Beasts

The Movie was ok. It was a typically Transformers Movie, that had not many Suprises.
Would not recommend unless you are a real Transformers Fan.

Spider-Man: A New Universe

This Movie was very good. The Movie is animated and seems to be more for children, but as Spider Man Fan it is a very intersting movie.
I would recommend if you are a Spider Man Fan and even more if you love the Spider Man Comics.

Spider-Man: Across the Spiderverse

This Movie was good. In this movie you can see the back story of the different Spider-Mans.
I can only recommend the movie if you have watched the first part (Spider-Man: A New Universe).
